About Us


Our Story

Luztow Logistics International Ltd was founded on the principle of providing efficient,reliable, and cost effective logistics solutions

to meet the growing demands of international trade. with our headquarters in Lagos, Nigeria. We have expanded our operations to other parts of the world, including the United States, Europe, Asia, and sub sahara Africa

Mission

Delivering fast, reliable logistics solutions across the globe.
